pre-sci­en­tif­ic work in physics LaTeX

Until late Feb­ru­ary I was oc­cu­pied in writ­ing and fin­ish­ing a work called “Fach­bere­ich­sar­beit” for my gram­mar school grad­u­a­tion. My the­sis has to do with cur­rent de­vel­op­ments and the­ses in the de­bate of the ac­cel­er­at­ing ex­pan­sion of the uni­verse and the­o­ries deal­ing with these top­ics. It gives also an overview on the his­to­ry of all the ob­ser­va­tions and dis­cus­sions that led us to the state of play.

Due to my cur­rent long-term and time-con­sum­ing task, the Matu­ra (Aus­tri­an gram­mar school grad­u­a­tion), it’s im­pos­si­ble for me to en­gage in other pro­jects by this time.

Par­ti­cle Zoo SVG

This is my try to vi­su­al­ize the par­ti­cles of the phys­i­cal stan­dard-model, show­ing not only the names and groups, but also some data like mass and spin. I cre­at­ed it as part of my pre-sci­en­tif­ic work men­tioned above.

Go to the de­tailed de­scrip­tion!

acry­lamid Python

acry­lamid is a com­pil­er for sta­t­ic blogs and home­pages and very easy to set up, use and ex­tend. It’s a pro­ject of posativ but I’m now deeply in­volved in the de­vel­ope­ment in con­junc­tion with him.

Lito­tex PHP

Lito­tex is a team work of the Lito­tex com­mu­ni­ty of which I am part of. It’s a PHP frame­work that has to be de­vel­opped so far, so that the code is read­able and the per­for­mance ac­cept­able. The con­cepts of DRY and mod­uliza­tion are the aim of that pro­ject.
And of course: It’s meant to make fun de­vel­op­ping (on) it.

Unity-Opera Python

Unity-Opera is a small Python-Script that adds Unity in­te­gra­tion to the Opera Web­brows­er.
Unity is the new Desk­top en­vi­ron­ment of the very pop­u­lar Linux dis­tri­b­u­tion Ubun­tu.

Since I’m using Gnome2/3 in­stead of Unity I stopped work­ing on it, but nev­er­the­less the script be­haves well with cur­rent ver­sions of Unity.

Ja­men­do-Down­loader Bash

This small Shell-Script can help you in en­larg­ing your music reper­toire! Just pass a tag (e.g. your fa­vorite genre), the album or an­oth­er list of music tracks or al­bums to the script and let them down­load in OGG or MP3. It uses the of­fi­cial API of Ja­men­do.

Batch-test your pro­grams Bash

This script ex­e­cutes one given pro­gram with a bunch of input files and com­pares the out­put with the so­lu­tion-files (if given). Very use­full when train­ing for the Olympiad in In­for­mat­ics, which pro­vides huge test­da­ta for every prob­lem.
